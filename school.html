About AIC Chebai Day Mixed Secondary School

Our School

Established in 1995, AIC Chebai Day Mixed Secondary School is a public day school institution located in Chebai Day, Kapcherop, Kenya. Affiliated with the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC), we are committed to nurturing academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills.

Mission

"To provide quality education through holistic development, fostering innovation, and empowering students to become responsible global citizens."

Vision

"To be a center of academic excellence that transforms lives and communities through education."

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Academic Excellence: Consistently high KCSE performance.
  • ✅ Qualified Teachers: Dedicated and experienced staff.
  • ✅ Modern Facilities: Well-equipped labs, library, and ICT center.
  • ✅ Extracurricular Activities: Sports, drama, music, and clubs.
  • ✅ Spiritual Growth: Chapel services and moral instruction.
  • ✅ Community Focus: Partnerships with local organizations.

Our Legacy

Founded in 1995 by Africa Inland Church (AIC), AIC Chebai Day has grown from a small community school to become one of Elgeyo Marakwet County's leading institutions. Our alumni include university scholars, professionals, and community leaders across Kenya.
